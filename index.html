About

ChordLearnerPlus generates randomly 10 questions to help improve your sight-reeding skills. All Questions/Scales/Chords are calculated from Scratch!

ChordQuestion

A chord will be asked for. Either Major or Minor. Please select the displayed chord in the canvas.

StepTriadQuestion

Please select the Step triad displayed in the Stave. Bonus Tip: The Kind of Scale can be deteremined by the number of Sharps/Flats infront of the Clef.

IntervalQuestion

Tired of not knowing your intervals? This Question will practice your sightreading skills.
